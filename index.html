Our Annual Mid-Autumn Party, The Lunar-Cup Tournament

We are thrilled to invite you to our Annual Mid-Autumn Party, The Lunar-Cup Tournament! As the full-moon graces the night sky, we will come together for an evening with laughter, fun puzzle challenges and the warm glow of lanterns guiding our way. Our scavenger hunt will take us on a journey through the spirit of this cherished festival, where each twist and turn brings us closer to victory. During ancient times, secret messages hidden in mooncakes helped the legendary king, Zhu Yuanzhang overthrow the Yuan dynasty, restoring peace to the land. This inspiring story will guide our festivities as we come together to celebrate tradition and friendship. Even Alto, will be there, ready to join the adventure. So gather your crew and prepare to set sail on this unforgettable journey, as our very own ship through time and heritage awaits!
